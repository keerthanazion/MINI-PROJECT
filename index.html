The JNTUH Health Centre serves as a pivotal healthcare hub catering to the needs of students, faculty, and staff of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH). Located within the campus, it offers a range of medical services including routine check-ups, emergency care, and specialized treatments. The centre is equipped with modern facilities and staffed by qualified medical professionals, ensuring timely and efficient healthcare delivery. It plays a crucial role in promoting wellness and providing medical support to the university community, fostering a healthy academic environment.

The JNTUH Health Centre provides essential healthcare services to students, staff, and residents of the JNTU quarters. Conveniently located on the JNTUH campus, it ensures easy access for the entire university community. The Health Centre operates with convenient timings, offering medical assistance throughout the week. This accessibility ensures that immediate and ongoing medical needs are met efficiently, promoting a healthy environment within the university.
